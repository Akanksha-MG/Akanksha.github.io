Health Insurance Analysis dashboard with tableau

The Health Insurance Analysis Dashboard, created using Tableau, offers a comprehensive view of health insurance claims data. This interactive platform provides insights into various aspects of health insurance, aiding in better decision-making and strategic planning. The dashboard features a scatter plot showing the correlation between claim amounts and age, highlighting potential age-related patterns. It includes a bar chart ranking the top 10 cities by total claim amounts, allowing users to identify geographic areas with the highest insurance claims. Another bar chart illustrates the impact of hereditary diseases on claim amounts, offering insights into how genetic conditions affect insurance costs. A pie chart compares claim amounts between smokers and non-smokers, underscoring the impact of smoking on health insurance claims. Additionally, a bar chart displays claim amounts by job title, revealing correlations between different occupations and insurance claims. The dashboard is interactive, with filters that let users explore specific segments of the dataset, such as smoker status and job title. This dynamic tool provides real-time updates based on applied filters, making it easy to gain tailored insights. Overall, the Health Insurance Analysis Dashboard empowers stakeholders with a deep understanding of health insurance claims, enhancing decision-making, operational efficiency, and risk management strategies.
